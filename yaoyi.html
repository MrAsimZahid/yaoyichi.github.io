Yao-Yi Chiang

I am an Associate Professor (Research) at the Spatial Sciences Institute, University of Southern California.

I build machine learning and data mining algorithms that incorporate spatial science techniques to handle spatial data. Some call this type of work GeoAI and others call it spatial computing. I also enjoy building working systems with my students and doing consulting work related to my research.

I teach data mining and advanced spatial computing at USC. I used to teach spatial databases and geospatial data integration.
